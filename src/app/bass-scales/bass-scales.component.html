Additional Tips:

It doesn't matter which actual 'note' you start on, it only matters that you have frets to land on for each of the numbers positions. It DOES matter that your bass is tuned to the standard EADG, or for 5 strings to BEADG

It also doesn't matter if you start at the top or the bottom; just remember to start on a red location, and if it's at the bottom, go from left-to-right reading upwards. If starting at the top, go from right-to-left, reading downward.
